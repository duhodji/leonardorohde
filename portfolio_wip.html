Spaceship Hallway

2023 | Cinema 4D

Leo created this video as his final project for a class on 3D animation at BMCC. The assighment was to make an animation that included Vents, support pllars, Pipes, turbines, an opening door, and a window. Leo chose make the sci-fi hallway that you can see. All textures are from Cinema 4D. The main challenge in this animation was to render the light properly.

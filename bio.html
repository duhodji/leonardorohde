LEONARDO ROHDE

Leo, as Leonardo Rohde is known, is game designer with a background and experience in animation and graphic desigh.

He is pursuing a Bachelor of Science Degree in Game Design and Development at the State University of New York (SUNY) in Canton, where he is has been consistently in the President's and Dean's Honors Lists. He also holds an Associate of Science Degree in Animation and Motion Graphics from the Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC), where he was a member of the Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society.

He has interned at Plot Kids, a maker of family-friendly games and animations; and at AnimaKing, an animation production company; both located in Florianópolis, Brazil.

Leo speaks English, Italian and Portuguese.
